Support

Need help?

We read every message. Reply usually within 24 hours.

Email us

The faster we can help, please include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your device (iPhone 15 Pro, Pixel 8, etc.) and OS version
  • The email you signed up with (so we can find your account)

Common questions

I didn't get my login email

Check your spam folder first. If it's not there, wait 60 seconds and try again. If still nothing after a few minutes, email us. We can manually verify the account.

The AI got my food wrong

Tap the meal, then tap the food item. You can edit calories, protein, carbs, fat, and portion size. Your edits are saved and used the next time you log the same food.

I want to delete my account

Open the app. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Confirm. Everything is wiped from our servers immediately.

If you cannot access the app for some reason, email us from the account's email address and we will delete it manually.

I can't log in

Try the "Forgot password" flow first. If that fails, email us. We can reset things on our side.

I want to export my data

Email us and ask. We will send you a JSON export of your food logs, weights, and goals within a few business days.

Calories are way off

The AI gives a confidence rating (high / medium / low) per food. Low confidence means it had to guess. Add more detail to your description (cooking method, portion size, brand) and re-log. Or edit the values directly.

I have feedback or a feature idea

Email us. We are a small team. Feedback actually changes what gets built.

Bug report

Found something broken? Email TrackKcal@gmail.com with the subject "Bug report" and include the steps to reproduce. Screenshots help.
