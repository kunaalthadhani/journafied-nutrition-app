Weekly Insights Premium

Your week, summarized.

Every Sunday, the AI writes a one-paragraph summary of how your week actually went. What worked. What didn't. What to try next.

Try it at launch All features →
What you see

One paragraph. That you'll actually read.

1

Calorie summary

Average daily intake vs goal. Banked calories. Days over and under. The simple math you'd want a coach to do for you.

2

Macro and micro highlights

Where you crushed it (protein on point all week). Where you slipped (vitamin D way down). Just the headline numbers.

3

Behavior callouts

Any pattern the AI noticed worth mentioning: weekend overeating, missing breakfasts, late dinners. Specific to your week.

Why this format

Charts aren't summaries.

Most apps drop you into a dashboard with six charts and call it "insights." That's data, not insight. A real coach would tell you, in words, what to take away.

The Weekly Insight is the coach version. Plain English. One screen. Then if you want to dig in, the charts are still there.

  • Delivered as a notification every Sunday morning
  • Cached so it doesn't change after first read
  • Previous weeks viewable in history
