Three ways in

Whichever is fastest in the moment.

1

Type

"Two manakeesh zaatar and a karak." Hit enter. Three seconds later you have a parsed meal with confidence ratings per item.

2

Talk

Hold the mic button. Say what you ate while you're walking from the kitchen. We transcribe with Whisper and feed it to the AI.

3

Snap

Photo of your plate. The AI reads what's on it and estimates portions. Useful for restaurants when you don't know exactly what you ordered.

Accuracy

The AI tells you how sure it is.

Every food gets a confidence badge: HIGH, MED, or LOW. High is within about 10 percent of reality. Low means there were assumptions you should see.

Edit any value once. The change is saved for next time you log the same food, so accuracy improves with use.

  • Confidence shown per food, not buried
  • AI's reasoning visible before you save
  • Edits sticky across sessions
