What it finds

The patterns that matter.

a

Timing patterns

"You overeat on days you logged dinner after 9 PM." Surfaces correlations between meal timing and total intake.

b

Day-of-week patterns

"Your weekend calories are 30% higher than weekdays." Highlights the cycles you might not see in a day-by-day chart.

c

Plateau triggers

"Your weight stalled the week your protein dropped below 100g." Connects nutrition shifts to weight outcomes.

How it runs

21 days. Quietly.

Every week, the AI looks at the last 21 days of food logs, weight entries, and goal adherence. It runs the math locally where possible, sends the rest through our proxy, and returns a short list of findings.

  • Runs in the background, no manual trigger needed
  • Shows new findings as a notification once weekly
  • Tap a finding to see the data behind it
  • Dismissed findings don't come back unless the pattern changes
