Smart Reminders Premium

Reminders that actually fire when you eat.

Most apps ping you at 8 AM whether you eat at 7 or 10. TrackKcal learns your timing and shows up when it's useful. Or if you'd rather pick your own times, that works too.

Try it at launch All features →
Two modes

Smart or Scheduled.

You pick. The toggle lives in Settings → Notifications.

1

Smart mode

The AI watches when you log meals over a few days. Detects your real breakfast, lunch, and dinner times. Reminds you 10 minutes before, if you haven't logged.

2

Scheduled mode

Pick your own times for breakfast, lunch, dinner. Toggle each on or off independently. Predictable and quiet.

3

Quiet hours

Set a do-not-disturb window. Reminders that would fall inside it are skipped, not pushed to a different time.

Bonus

End-of-day wrap-up.

Optional 8:30 PM notification that summarizes what you ate, how close you got to your macros, and whether the day went the way you planned. Brief. No guilt.

  • One tap to open today's full breakdown
  • Skipped if you've already logged a full day
  • Adjustable time so it fits your evening
