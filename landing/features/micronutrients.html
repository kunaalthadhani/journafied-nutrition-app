What gets tracked

Twelve that matter.

Not every nutrient under the sun. The ones that actually affect how you feel.

Mi

Minerals

Iron, calcium, magnesium, zinc, sodium, potassium. Low iron makes you tired. Low magnesium gives you cramps. You should know your numbers.

Vi

Vitamins

Vitamin A, C, D, B12. The big ones. Vitamin D especially matters indoors, which is most of us most of the time.

Fa

Fats and fiber

Omega-3, dietary fiber, saturated fat, sugar (including added sugars). The numbers that change how your body feels day to day.

How it's calculated

Per food. Per day. Per week.

When the AI parses your meal, it estimates micros for each food along with the macros. You get a daily total, a weekly average, and a comparison to recommended ranges for your age and sex.

  • Daily average view with confidence ranges
  • Color-coded: green if hitting, amber if low, red if very low
  • 7, 30, 90 day windows so noise gets filtered out
