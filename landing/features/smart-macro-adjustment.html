How it works

It watches. You decide.

1

Tracks your trend

Looks at your weight over the last 14 to 28 days. Filters out daily noise. Compares actual progress to your goal trajectory.

2

Suggests an adjustment

If you are losing too fast, it suggests adding calories. Too slow, it suggests cutting. Always explained, never silent.

3

You confirm or skip

Nothing changes without your tap. Adjustments arrive as a banner, not a forced update. Skip it and the suggestion comes back when the trend persists.

Why it matters

Most apps never adjust.

You set a goal in week one. You eat the same target for a year. Your metabolism adapts, your body changes, your goal updates. The target stays frozen. Then you wonder why progress stalled.

Smart Macro Adjustment closes that loop without forcing you back to a calculator every month.

  • Threshold adjustable: how aggressive should the suggestion be
  • History view shows every past adjustment
  • Macros (protein / carbs / fat) update together, not just calories
