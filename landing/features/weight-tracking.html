What you get

Ten insight cards.

Not just a chart with a line on it.

1

Goal Progress

Where you started, where you are, where you're heading. Visual progress bar with estimated goal date based on current trajectory.

2

Weekly Rate

How fast you're actually losing or gaining. Compared to a healthy range. Flag if you're moving too fast either way.

3

Plateau detection

If your weight stops moving for 14+ days, the app says so explicitly. Then it suggests what to look at: protein, calorie creep, sleep, stress.

4

Weight vs Calories

Side-by-side chart showing weight trend overlaid on average daily intake. The relationship between input and output, visible.

5

Fluctuation Range

Daily highs and lows over the past 30 days. Normalizes the panic of waking up 1.5 kg heavier on a Tuesday.

6

Milestones

Auto-celebrates the moments worth marking: first 1 kg, 5 kg, halfway to goal, goal reached.

Locked until ready

Insights unlock as you log.

Some of the 10 cards need a minimum of data to be meaningful. Plateau detection needs 14 days. Weekly rate needs 7. Goal progress is instant.

Locked cards show what's missing, so you know what to do next.
