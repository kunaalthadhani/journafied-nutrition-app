What you can ask

It's your history talking.

"Why am I tired this week?"

It checks your iron, your sleep notes if you log them, your protein, your weight. Answers from your data, not a generic blog.

"What should I eat to hit my protein?"

Limits

What it won't do.

It is not a doctor. It will not diagnose. It will not replace a registered dietitian if you have a medical condition. It is a tool that knows your numbers and explains them.

  • Free: 7 messages per day
  • Premium: 10 messages per day, plus pattern-aware nudges
  • Does not remember conversations across sessions on purpose, so each answer is grounded in current data
