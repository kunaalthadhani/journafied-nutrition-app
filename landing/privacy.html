Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data TrackKcal collects, why we collect it, and what we do with it. We try to keep this in plain English. If something is unclear, email us at TrackKcal@gmail.com and we will fix it.

Who we are

TrackKcal is a mobile application owned and operated by Kunaal Thadhani, based in the United Arab Emirates. We can be reached at TrackKcal@gmail.com.

What we collect

Account information

When you sign up we collect your email address. If you choose to add a name or phone number, we collect those too. We do not collect your physical address.

Health and nutrition data you give us

If you choose to use the app, you give us:

  • Food log entries (what you ate, when, the AI's estimated calories and nutrients)
  • Weight entries (the values you record, dated)
  • Goals you set (target weight, target calories, dietary preferences)
  • Exercise entries you log
  • Optional messages you send to the in-app AI Nutritionist

This information is stored against your account. You can edit or delete any entry at any time inside the app.

Usage and device data

We collect anonymous information about how you use the app: which screens you open, how often, which features you tap, app version, operating system, and device model. This helps us decide what to build and fix. It is not tied to your identity beyond your user ID.

Crash and error data

When the app crashes or hits an error, we collect the crash details so we can fix the bug. This may include the screen you were on and any non-sensitive context about the action that failed. We do not include your food log content in crash reports.

How we use your data

  • To run the app. Save your meals, sync across your devices, show your history.
  • To analyze food you describe. The text or photo you submit is sent to our AI processing service so it can return nutrition estimates.
  • To improve the product. We look at usage patterns in aggregate to decide what to build next.
  • To send you reminders. Only if you turn notifications on. We do not send marketing emails by default.
  • To respond to you. If you email us, we read it and reply.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not build a profile of you for any third party.

Who else sees your data

We use a small number of trusted providers to run the app. Each one only sees the data needed to do its job.

  • Supabase hosts our database and handles authentication. Your account record and your logged data live here. Supabase is operated by Supabase Inc., a US-incorporated company with infrastructure in multiple regions.
  • OpenAI runs the AI models that analyze food descriptions, photos, and voice notes. When you log a meal, the description or image is sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI states that data submitted via its API is not used to train their models.
  • Sentry receives crash and error reports. Our Sentry instance is hosted in the European Union region.
  • Mixpanel receives anonymized usage events so we can measure how the app is being used.
  • Apple and Google handle app distribution and, if and when we enable paid tiers, payment processing.

Where your data lives

Our database is hosted in Asia-Pacific (Seoul). Crash reports are stored in the European Union. AI processing happens on servers operated by OpenAI in the United States. By using TrackKcal you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in these regions.

How long we keep it

We keep your account and your data for as long as you have an account. If you delete your account inside the app, we delete:

  • Your food, weight, exercise, and goal entries
  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Any reminders or preferences you set

Anonymized usage data and crash reports may persist in our analytics and error tracking systems after account deletion because those records cannot be tied back to you.

Your rights

You can:

  • See your data by opening the app
  • Edit or delete individual entries at any time
  • Delete your entire account from inside the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Email us with any other request: TrackKcal@gmail.com

If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom, you also have rights under GDPR to access, correct, port, or restrict processing of your personal data. Email us and we will help.

Children

TrackKcal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, email us and we will delete it.

Security

We use modern security practices to protect your data. Your password is hashed. Connections to our servers use HTTPS. Database access requires authentication. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data carefully.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. For material changes we will also notify you inside the app or by email.

Contact

Questions, complaints, or requests: TrackKcal@gmail.com
